2. Card 1: The Illusion of Free Time (ការបំភាន់នៃពេលវេលាទំនេរ):

The Reality: In high school, you are in class 7 hours a day. At university, you might only be in class 3 hours a day.

The Trap: It feels like you have infinite free time, but university requires 2 to 3 hours of independent reading and studying for every 1 hour you spend in a lecture hall. You are now your own manager.

3. Card 2: The Syllabus is the Law (គម្រោងកម្មវិធីសិក្សាគឺជាច្បាប់):

Explain what a Syllabus (Syllabus/គម្រោងកម្មវិធីសិក្សា) is.

In high school, teachers remind you when homework is due. At university, the professor hands you a Syllabus on Day 1 with every due date for the next 4 months. They will never remind you. If you miss a deadline, you fail the assignment.
