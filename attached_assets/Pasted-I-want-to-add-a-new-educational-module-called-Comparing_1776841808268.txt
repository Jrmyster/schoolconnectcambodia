I want to add a new educational module called 'Comparing the World: Comparative & Superlative Adjectives' (ការប្រៀបធៀបពិភពលោក៖ គុណនាមប្រៀបធៀប និងកម្រិតខ្ពស់បំផុត) to the English Writing section. Please implement the following cleanly:1. The Core Concept (គោលគំនិតសំខាន់):Explain the three levels of measurement:Base Adjective: Describes one thing (The elephant is big).Comparative (-er / more): Compares exactly TWO things (The elephant is bigger than the dog).Superlative (-est / most): Compares THREE OR MORE things (The elephant is the biggest animal on land).2. The Rules of Spelling (ច្បាប់នៃការប្រកប):Use a clean, visually distinct set of cards to break down the rules:Rule 1 (Short Words - 1 Syllable): Add '-er' and '-est'. (Fast $\rightarrow$ Faster $\rightarrow$ Fastest).Rule 2 (Ending in 'e'): Just add '-r' and '-st'. (Large $\rightarrow$ Larger $\rightarrow$ Largest).Rule 3 (Consonant-Vowel-Consonant): Double the last letter! (Hot $\rightarrow$ Hotter $\rightarrow$ Hottest).Rule 4 (Ending in 'y'): Drop the 'y', change to 'i', add '-er' / '-est'. (Happy $\rightarrow$ Happier $\rightarrow$ Happiest).Rule 5 (Long Words - 2+ Syllables): Do not change the word! Use 'more' and 'most' in front of it. (Beautiful $\rightarrow$ More beautiful $\rightarrow$ Most beautiful).Rule 6 (The Rule Breakers): Irregular words that must be memorized. (Good $\rightarrow$ Better $\rightarrow$ Best) and (Bad $\rightarrow$ Worse $\rightarrow$ Worst).3.
