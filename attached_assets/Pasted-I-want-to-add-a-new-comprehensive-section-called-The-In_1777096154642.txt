The Lipid Envelope: Many dangerous viruses (like the Flu and COVID-19) are protected by an oily outer shell (a lipid bilayer).

3. The Rogues Gallery (បញ្ជីវីរុសទូទៅ):

Create a categorized, responsive grid of cards displaying the most common human viruses. Use this exact data and ensure headings are bilingual (English/Khmer):

Respiratory Viruses (វីរុសផ្លូវដង្ហើម):

Influenza A & B: Causes the seasonal flu.

SARS-CoV-2: Causes COVID-19.

Rhinovirus: The primary cause of the common cold.

Respiratory Syncytial Virus (RSV): Causes severe respiratory infections, especially dangerous for children and the elderly.

Adenovirus: Causes respiratory issues, conjunctivitis (pink eye), or fever.

Gastrointestinal and Liver Viruses (វីរុសក្រពះពោះវៀន និងថ្លើម):

Norovirus: Causes highly contagious viral gastroenteritis ('stomach flu').

Rotavirus: A very common cause of severe diarrhea in children.

Hepatitis A, B, C, D, and E: Viruses that cause inflammation and damage to the liver.

Common Skin and Systemic Viruses (វីរុសស្បែក និងប្រព័ន្ធរាងកាយ):

Herpes Simplex Virus (HSV-1 & HSV-2): Causes oral and genital herpes.

Varicella-Zoster Virus (VZV): Causes chickenpox in children and shingles in adults.

Human Papillomavirus (HPV): Causes warts and is directly linked to certain cancers.

Epstein-Barr Virus (EBV): Causes infectious mononucleosis.

Blood-Borne and Other Viruses (វីរុសក្នុងឈាម និងផ្សេងៗ):

HIV (Human Immunodeficiency Virus): Attacks the immune system and causes AIDS.

Dengue/Zika Virus: Dangerous mosquito-borne viruses prevalent in tropical climates.

Rabies Virus: A fatal virus transmitted by animal bites that attacks the central nervous system.
