Arc Welding & Shielding Gas: Explain that electric welding uses an arc of electricity to create heat. It uses 'Shielding Gases' like Argon or CO2 to push away the air. If Oxygen from the air touches the hot liquid metal, it will create bubbles and make the weld weak (Oxidation).

4. Card 3: The Metals (ប្រភេទលោហៈ):

Steel (ដែក): The most common metal for welding due to its strength and abundance.

Stainless Steel (ដែកអ៊ីណុក): Requires specialized gas and care to prevent it from losing its rust-resistant properties.

5. Card 4: How to Weld (របៀបផ្សារ):
