Explain the simple rules of the '3x + 1' problem:

Pick ANY positive number.

If it is EVEN, divide it by 2. (បើសិនជាលេខគូ ចែកវាដោយ ២)

If it is ODD, multiply by 3 and add 1. (បើសិនជាលេខសេស គុណនឹង ៣ រួចបូក ១)
