Moving into Module 3: Energy & Thermodynamics (ថាមពល និងទែម៉ូឌីណាមិច) is a transition into the "Currency" of the universe. In General Semantics, "Energy" is a high-level abstraction—you can't see energy itself, only its effects (motion, light, or heat).

From a biological perspective, you are a Heat Engine. Your body converts chemical energy from food into kinetic energy (movement) and thermal energy (body heat). By learning thermodynamics, students learn the fundamental laws that govern their own metabolism and the machines that power Cambodia's development.

1. Types of Energy (ប្រភេទថាមពល):

Kinetic Energy (ថាមពលស៊ីនេទិច): The energy of motion. If it’s moving, it has kinetic energy!

Potential Energy (ថាមពលប៉ូតង់ស្យែល): Stored energy. A rock at the top of a hill has the 'potential' to fall.

2. The First Law: Conservation (ច្បាប់រក្សាថាមពល):

English: Energy cannot be created or destroyed; it only changes form. When you clap your hands, kinetic energy turns into sound and heat.
